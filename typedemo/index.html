The Official Guide to Being a Spooky Scary Skeleton

  1. Be creaky. No one finds uncreaky skeletons scary. If you're not old enough for your bones to creak much yet, do some noises with your mouth and no one will be able to tell the difference.
  2. Perfect the jaw dropping. It's really scary when skeletons dropping their jaws open and they kinda swing a little.
  3. Learn your holidays! Skeletons are a big part of holidays like Halloween and Dia de los Muertos, know when you're needed!
  4. Don't get intimidated by those cheap lawn decorations that people put out for Halloween. You're so much better than those pretenders!
  5. Accept your immortality. Now that you're a skelton, you're gonna be around a while, better get used to it.
