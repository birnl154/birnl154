the data that is featured in this project shows the number of homes that reported seeing rodents in 2017. the data set includes all the sub-boroughs in nyc and the city as a whole. the data set has been collected by the US Census Bureau through the NYC Housing and Vacancy Survey, and by the NYC Health Department through Community Health Surveys. for display purposes, each rat emoji represents 1000 home sightings. for example, 6 rat emojis means 6000 homes that reported sightings.
