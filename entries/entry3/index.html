Week Three




WORDS INSPIRED BY THIS WEEK'S GIF TO USE IN A POEM:

Cake, Hat, Big jeans, Tight top, Plastic spoon



week three poem



Blow the candles/when you count to ten/wish for something
You wanted then/wearing big jeans/and a tight top
For big dreams/and a rooftop/break plastic spoons
On chocolate cake/libras moons/and time to take
Slow life down/to birthday hats,
Birthday clowns/and birthday laughs.
