Week Three Poem
Bodoni Mota: A font designed by Giambattisa Bodoni in 1798. It is used for movie posters and logos, including the famous “CK” for Calvin Klein, a famous fashion house.


Blow the candles
Cutive Mono: A font depicted below that is in areas with a large body of text or poetry

When you count to ten

Wish for something

You wanted then

Wearing big jeans

And a tight top

For big dreams

And a rooftop

Break plastic spoons

On chocolate cake

Libras moons

And time to take

Slow life down

To birthday hats,

Birthday clowns

And birthday laughs.
